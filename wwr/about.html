History

Founded in 2010 along the banks of the mighty Vaal River, Carnival City White Water Rafting began with a simple dream — to share the thrill and beauty of white water rafting with adventurers of all ages. What started as a small family-run business with just two rafts and a passion for the outdoors quickly grew into one of the most trusted rafting operators in the region.
